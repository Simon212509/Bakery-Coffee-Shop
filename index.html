Welcome to Roll

Freshly Baked Goodness

We craft fresh, handmade pastries, breads, and expertly brewed coffee every day.

Experience the Warmth

At Roll, we take pride in our time-honored baking techniques and commitment to quality. Every morning, the aroma of freshly baked bread and rich coffee fills our space, welcoming our customers to slow down and enjoy the moment.

Our selection of artisan treats changes daily, ensuring there’s always something new to discover. From buttery croissants to perfectly balanced espresso, we pay attention to every detail. We also support sustainability by sourcing locally and using eco-friendly packaging.

Join us for a cup of coffee, a flaky pastry, or a conversation with friends. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or settling in with a book, we want you to feel at home.
